Guess the Disney Character

Think of a character, answer the clues, and the game will rank its best guess in real time.

48 characters

Live Read

How it decides

Each answer adjusts every character's score. The next clue is picked to split the strongest remaining candidates.

Answer nuance

Yes and No are strong signals. Maybe is soft evidence. I don't know skips the clue without penalty.

Keyboard play

Use Y for yes, N for no, M for maybe, I for I don't know, U to undo, and R to restart.
