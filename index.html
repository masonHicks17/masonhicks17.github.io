How I Build with AI

AI is my thinking partner, not my replacement. Here's how I use it throughout my process:

01

Brainstorm & Ideate

I start with a problem or creative spark. AI helps me explore possibilities, ask better questions, and refine the core idea before any code.

02

Wireframe & Design

I sketch ideas on paper, then use AI to generate design concepts, explore layouts, and iterate on visual direction quickly.

03

Generate First Pass

AI generates initial code and components. I review, understand, and improve—never blindly copying. This accelerates the "getting started" phase significantly.

04

Refine & Personalize

I take the foundation and shape it into something thoughtful. This is where the design system comes together and the product gets personality.

05

Debug & Polish

When bugs surface, AI helps me think through the problem, find edge cases, and optimize. I validate all solutions before shipping.

06

Ship & Iterate

I deploy early and gather feedback. AI helps me process feedback and plan the next iteration, but I decide what matters.
